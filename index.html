[ 02 ]

Launch sequence

T-00 · CONNECT

Wire up a device

Enable USB debugging, plug in over USB or pair over Wi-Fi. Vortynix detects it instantly — no app to install on the phone.

T-01 · LAUNCH

Spin up instances

Pick apps across every connected device and open them. Each one lands in its own tab on its own virtual display.

T-02 · ARM

Wire your controls

Map zone shortcuts, set up broadcast, balance audio per device, and save layouts you can restore in one click.

T-03 · PILOT

Fly the whole fleet

Run everything from one deck — switch, control, and automate a dozen instances without ever leaving the window.
